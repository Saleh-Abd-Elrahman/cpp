std::multimap< int, std::string, std::greater<> > zipF::sortFrequencies ( const std::map< std::string, int > & frequencies)

Sorts words by their frequency, from most to least frequent.

  • This function takes in a map of words (keys) and their frequencies (values).

It creates a new sorted list of words, organized by frequency in descending order.

  • Words with the same frequency are grouped together.
    Parameters
    frequenciesMap of words and their corresponding frequencies.
    Returns
    Multimap with frequencies as keys and words as values.
